How to Get Discord Bot Token

This guide will show you how to create a Discord application and get your bot token from the Discord Developer Portal.

Go to Discord Developer Portal

1
Visit the Discord Developer Portal

Open the Discord Developer Portal in your browser and log in with your Discord account.

Discord Developer Portal homepage

Create Your Application

2
Click "New Application" or select an existing one

Click the "New Application" button to create a new Discord application for your bot.

Create Application dialog with name field and ToS agreement
3
Enter application name and agree to terms

Give your application a name, check the agreement boxes, and click "Create".

Application created with name entered and agreements checked

Navigate to Bot Section

4
Click on "Bot" in the left sidebar

In your application settings, navigate to the "Bot" section in the left sidebar.

Bot section in the left sidebar

Get Your Bot Token

5
Click "Reset Token" or "Copy" to get your bot token

On the Bot page under Token, click "Reset Token" to generate a new bot token.

You won't be able to view your token again unless you regenerate it, so make sure to keep it somewhere safe (like in a password manager)

Reset Token button in the Bot section
Important

You may be asked to verify your identity with 2FA or password when resetting the token.

6
Copy your bot token

After verification, your bot token will be displayed. Click "Copy" to copy it to your clipboard.

Next Steps

Now that you have your bot token, you can:

  • Use it in your bot code to connect to Discord
  • Set up bot permissions and intents
  • Invite your bot to servers
  • Start developing your bot's functionality
